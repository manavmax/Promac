Electrical Safety Resources

Essential safety protocols and compliance guidelines for Indian electrical systems

Critical Safety Alert

Always turn off main power supply before starting any electrical work. Use proper PPE and follow lockout/tagout procedures.

Personal Protective Equipment

  • • Insulated gloves (Class 0 minimum)
  • • Safety glasses with side shields
  • • Non-conductive footwear
  • • Arc-rated clothing for high voltage

Indian Standards Compliance

  • • IS 732: Code of Practice for Electrical Wiring
  • • IS 3043: Code for Earthing Practice
