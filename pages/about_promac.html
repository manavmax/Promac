Our Journey Through Time

Milestones that shaped India's electrical landscape

1999
The Beginning

Founded as a small electrical components supplier in Mumbai, Promac began with a vision to provide quality electrical products to the growing Indian market. Our first warehouse stocked basic switches and fuses for local electricians.

2003
ISI Certification

Achieved ISI certification for our first product line of modular switches. This milestone established our commitment to quality and safety standards, setting the foundation for future expansion across India.

2008
National Expansion

Opened manufacturing facilities in Delhi and Bangalore, expanding our reach to serve major metropolitan areas. Introduced LED lighting solutions and smart switching technology to the Indian market.

2015
Digital Innovation

Launched India's first IoT-enabled electrical switches and comprehensive home automation systems. Established our R&D center in Pune to drive innovation in smart electrical solutions.

2020
Sustainability Focus

Committed to carbon neutrality by 2030. Launched the Green Electrical Initiative, focusing on energy-efficient products and sustainable manufacturing processes across all facilities.

2025
Future Ready

Leading India's transition to smart cities with comprehensive electrical infrastructure solutions. Serving 500+ cities with advanced automation, renewable energy integration, and AI-powered electrical systems.
